### CC BY-SA (Creative Commons Attribution-ShareAlike)

### CC0 / Public Domain
- ✅ No restrictions
- ✅ No attribution required
- ✅ Can use however you want

### Unsplash / Pexels License
- ✅ Free for commercial and non-commercial use
- ✅ No permission needed
- ✅ No attribution required (but appreciated)

## Attribution in Story Map

When using photos in the Story Map:

**For images requiring attribution:**
- Add caption with photographer name and license
- Example: "Jim Corbett National Park. Photo by John Doe, CC BY-SA 4.0"

**For images not requiring attribution:**
- Still good practice to credit
- Example: "Kaziranga National Park. Photo by Jane Smith"
